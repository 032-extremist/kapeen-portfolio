I'm Daniel — a final-year Information Technology student at Murang'a University of Technology, graduating in 2027. I split my time across three things that turn out to be more connected than they look: writing software, making sense of data, and understanding the networks that move it all around.

I like projects that force me to touch the full stack of a problem — from a database schema, to a model that has to actually be right, to the cabling-and-config layer most developers never think about.
